How to use iRoom

A step by step guide to meeting your friends in iRoom

This is the homepage of iRoom. If you already have a link to a Room,
please click on "join room". If you want to create a new Room,
please click on "start room"

If you have clicked on "start room",
this window will appear.
You can click on "copy link" to copy
and send the link to the people who you
want to be in your Room with.
Please note, that you can always
return to the homepage by clicking on
the iRoom logo in the top left corner.

If you have clicked on "join room",
this site will emerge.
You can now copy the link you have
recieved from your host and click on
"join room" again to enter the room. If you want to create a new Room,
you once again have the opportunity to
click on "start room".

This is your Room!
You can talk to people by dragging you
image towards them until you can hear them loud enough.
You can also navigate with your direction keys.
The Team of iRoom wishes you a good session. Have fun!
